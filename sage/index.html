Sage is an app that helps you improve at League of Legends

PERSONALCOACHING

How it works

01.

MATCH REVIEWS BY PROFESSIONAL PLAYERS

Choose which of your matches to submit for review by our network of professional League of Legends players

02.

GET PERSONAL FEEDBACK

One of our coaches will compile video feedback on your match, providing insights on how you can improve

03.

EXPLORE AND LEARN

Search other player’s reviews and learn about different champions, roles, combos, and more

04.

HELP OTHER PLAYERS

Join a community of League of Legends players that want to improve. Comment on other player’s reviews, offer advice, and earn karma
